About Me

I left the Army in May of 2018 and moved to Denver Colorado. After meeting some amazing people they pushed me to try a free one day course at a local bootcamp. I was hooked. My experience and desire to problem solve as well as my passion to help others and work in an ongoing learning environment fueled me to seek out a top notch bootcamp to cultivate my skills and become a software developer. I packed up my things in Denver, sold my home and moved to Nashville in May of 2020 to attend Nashville Software School.
